Frittata Recipe

Description

Frittatas are a great make-ahead breakfast. This recipe will show you how to make a great frittata, if you can afford the eggs.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup any milk
  • 2 small garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • black pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes
  • feta or another crumbly cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Mix the eggs, milk, and salt until well combined.
  2. Heat 1/2 tablespoon olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add tomatoes and garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Then add the egg mixture.
  4. Distribute the egg mixture throughout the pan and then add the cheese on top.
  5. Transfer to the oven and bake 15 to 20 minutes until eggs are set.
  6. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil.
