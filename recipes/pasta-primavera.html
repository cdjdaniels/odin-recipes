Pasta Primavera Recipe

Description

Pasta primavera in spring is an immaculate thing. And a healthy one.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry rigatoni or penne pasta/li>
  • olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped asparagus
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped/li>
  • 1 medium zucchini, chopped
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 medium shallot
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 grated lemon peel
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup oat milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese or nutritional yeast
  • fresh basil and parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions
  2. Add oil to a skillet over medium heat. Stir in vegetables and cook for 7 minutes, covered. Add salt and pepper to taste and set aside.
  3. To make the sauce, put oil in the same skillet. stir in shallot and cook 3 minutes. Stir in garlic and lemon peel for an additional minute.
  4. Stir in vegetable broth and cook uncovered for 8 minutes.
  5. Stir in oat milk, lemon juice, and cheese or yeast. Stir and cook until the sauce is hot.
  6. Assemble the cooked pasta and vegetables and coat with sauce. Add fresh basil and parsley leaves, plus additional nutritional yeast or parmesan to garnish.
