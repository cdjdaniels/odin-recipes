Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is the best food in the world, that is just an objective fact. This recipe will tell you how to create the perfect neapolitan style margherita pizza with gooey mozzarella cheese, rich tomato sauce, thin crust, and fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • 1 can San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 small garlic cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • cornmeal
  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Place the tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, and salt in a food processor and process until smooth.
  3. Dust a baking sheet or pizza stone with cornmeal.
  4. Place the pizza dough onto the baking sheet and stretch it to desired size.
  5. Spread tomato sauce over pizza dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  6. Top with mozzarella and transfer to oven. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until crust is browned.
  7. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil.
